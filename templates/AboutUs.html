Company Story

A next-generation global technology firm that was founded in 2005, RM Employee Management assists companies of all sizes and across all sectors in modernising their HR functions to increase efficiency.

The firm initially had a Windows-based HR System, but as time went on, both its reputation and its clientele increased. We identified an opportunity to transition from Window-based software to a Cloud Human Resource Management Software (HRMS) when the economy and trends changed, and the Cloud-Based HRMS software was successfully introduced in India in 2014. The business kept up its growth and entered China, Thailand, and Cambodia.

As of 2022, Info-Tech has a staff of more than 300 people, serves more than 20,000 clients, and administers the payroll for more than 500,000 workers.

Our Services

We help to make managing businesses easy by providing an All-in-one solution to manage human resource matters.

Our Human Resource Management System(HRMS) provides a complete solution which includes manage employee information and leaves.
